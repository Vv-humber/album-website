Overview

Dissolving Light is the first album by Bleeding Ink. Released in 2024 by Silver Crescent Records.

Dissolving Light dives into the Mysterious World of the Occult, with their music taking a turn towards preaching the Band's values and teachings.

Serving followers with a place free from the world and with songs that comforting (and sligtly manipulate) listeners into the night.
